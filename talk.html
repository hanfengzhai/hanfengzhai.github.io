    Designing next generation biocompatible materials with Machine Learning + Multiscale & Multiphysics Modeling, 2022.
  • Contributed poster for Cornell admitted Ph.D. students visiting week, at 4th floor Upson.
  • The overall goal is to design materials for long-term biofilm control.
  • We developed an automated Bayesian optimization workflow for designing such materials.
